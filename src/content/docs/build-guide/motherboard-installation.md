## Introduction

This chapter installs the prepared Gigabyte B850 AORUS Elite WiFi7 motherboard into the Lian Li O11 Dynamic Mini V2 Flow.

## Purpose

Secure the motherboard in the case without flexing the PCB, trapping cables, or leaving incorrect standoff contact points.

## Estimated Time

20-35 minutes.

## Difficulty

Moderate.

## Required Tools

- Phillips #2 screwdriver.
- Case motherboard screws.
- Flashlight.
- Anti-static wrist strap.

## Warnings

- Do not drag the underside of the motherboard across standoffs.
- Do not overtighten motherboard screws.
- Do not trap front-panel, USB, fan, or PSU cables under the board.
- Do not install the GPU before the motherboard screws are checked.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Confirm CPU, RAM, and SSD are already installed on the motherboard.
2. Confirm the case has the correct ATX standoff layout.
3. Check that the integrated rear I/O shield area is clear.
4. Hold the motherboard by its edges.
5. Lower the board into the case, aligning the rear I/O with the case opening.
6. Align the screw holes over the standoffs.
7. Start the centre screw first if the case has a locating standoff; otherwise start a corner screw lightly.
8. Install all required motherboard screws loosely.
9. Tighten screws in stages until snug.
10. Connect the 24-pin ATX motherboard power cable.
11. Connect CPU EPS power at the top-left of the motherboard.
12. Leave front-panel, USB, audio, fan, pump, and RGB connections for the dedicated connector chapters.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Rear I/O is aligned correctly.
- [ ] Every installed screw enters a standoff.
- [ ] No screw is cross-threaded.
- [ ] No unused standoff touches the underside of the board.
- [ ] 24-pin ATX power is fully seated.
- [ ] CPU EPS power is fully seated.
- [ ] Motherboard is level and secure.

## Common Mistakes

- Forgetting the CPU EPS connector until access is blocked.
- Overtightening screws and stressing the PCB.
- Leaving the rear I/O misaligned.
- Dropping screws onto the motherboard.
- Using PSU screws instead of motherboard screws.

## Expected Result

The motherboard is mounted securely in the case with main board power connected.
